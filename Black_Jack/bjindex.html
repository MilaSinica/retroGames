Help

Press "DEAL" to play a new hand. You can hit, stand and double in a hand. The aim of the game is to beat dealer`s hand while staying below 21. If you have higher score than the dealer and not bust, you win the hand. "HIT" deals you one card, you may hit 5 times. Your point is not going over 21. "STAND" button ends your turn. You can stand if you want no more cards. All face cards are worth 10. An ace is worth 11 unless the hand would exceed 21 - in which case an ace is worth 1. Win a hand to receive a bet score to your balance. If dealer wins - you`ll lose it. Press "DOUBLE" to double a bet. You can double only one time - before you hit. The game is over after you have a 0 balance. Reset game to renew a balance.

Good Luck!
